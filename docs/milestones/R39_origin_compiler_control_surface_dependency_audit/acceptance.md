# R39 Acceptance

- the lane stays same-substrate and same-opcode throughout;
- the comparator set is fixed to baseline plus one declared helper-body
  permutation before execution;
- the lane tests compiler-control dependence rather than a new family;
- the result reports exact trace/exact final-state and
  baseline-versus-perturbation comparisons separately;
- the perturbation preserves final state while changing trace on the declared
  rows;
- broader compiler, arbitrary-language, or frontier claims remain blocked even
  if the audit is locally positive.
