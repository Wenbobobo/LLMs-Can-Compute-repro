# R52 Stop Conditions

Stop and report a negative or mixed result if any are true:

1. the internal accelerated route loses exactness against the internal
   reference;
2. the internal route shows no bounded value over the external baseline on the
   declared rows;
3. the measured advantage depends on hidden exclusions or incomparable setups;
   or
4. the lane drifts into broad systems marketing instead of bounded comparator
   evidence.
